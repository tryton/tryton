******
Design
******

The *Production Ethanol Module* introduces or extends the following concepts.

.. _model-production:

Production
==========

When the *Production Ethanol Module* is activated, the *Production* gains a new
property that calculates the volume of alcohol gained or lost.

.. seealso::

   The `Production <production:model-production>` concept is introduced by the
   :doc:`Production Module <production:index>`.
